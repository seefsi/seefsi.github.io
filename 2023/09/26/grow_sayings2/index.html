美國士兵守則：散戶投資指南

2023-09-26
  1. 内容：

不理解的标红，非常理解的标绿，不重要的标灰。有需要的加说明，方便以后查看。

内容：

You are not a superman.
你不是巴菲特。

If it’s stupid but works, it isn’t stupid.
如果一種笨方法能讓你到賺錢，那就不是笨辦法。

Don’t look conspicuous – it draws fire.
赚钱了别嘚瑟，混久了总是要还的。

Never share a foxhole with anyone braver than you are.
永遠不要與比你衝動的人買同一隻股票。

Never forget that the lowest bidder made your weapon.
別忘了，券商也在割你的韭菜。

If your attack is going really well, it’s an ambush.
如果你覺得股市裡賺錢很容易，那就離虧錢不遠了。

All five-second grenade fuses will burn down in three seconds.
所有跌了50%的票，都會再跌50%。

Try to look unimportant because bad guys may be low on ammo.
儘量保持低調，否則你会被 $TSLA 腦殘粉攻擊的！

If you are forward of your position, the artillery will fall short.
如果你的票漲多了，那說明它快跌了。

The enemy diversion you are ignoring is the main attack.
看不見的風險，才是真風險。

The important things are always simple.
重要的事總是很簡單。

The simple things are always hard.
簡單的東西總是很難。

The easy way is always mined.
別試圖走捷徑，你會踩雷的。

If you are short of everything except enemy. You are in combat.
如果你除了虧錢，什麼也沒得到，那你一定是在炒幣。

Incoming fire has the right of way.
下跌的時候不要談價值，按圖操作即可。

If the enemy is in range, SO ARE YOU!!!
盈虧同源，懂不？

No combat ready unit has ever passed inspections.
沒有任何一個買入決定是無懈可擊的。

Things that must be together to work usually can’t be shipped together.
基本面，消息面和技術面得出的結論，永遠不會統一。

Radios will fail as soon as you need fire support desperately.
最需要抄底的時候，往往沒有資金了。

Anything you do can get you shot – including doing nothing.
股市裡，你不論做什麼都可能虧錢，包括空倉。

Tracers work both ways.
你的交易對手，也在看與你一樣的信息。

The only thing more accurate than incoming enemy fire is incoming friendly fire.
比騙子更容易讓你虧錢的，是朋友推薦的股票。

Make it tough for the enemy to get in and you can’t get out.
如果你不願意承擔風險，那你也賺不到大錢。

If you take more than your fair share of objectives, you will have more than your fair share of objectives to take.
如果你賺了不屬於自己的錢，遲早會連屬於你的本金都虧回去。

When both sides are convinced that they are about to lose, they are both right.
當多頭和空頭都撐不下去的時候，拐點就來了。

Professional soldiers are predictable but the world is full of amateurs.
真正的投資者是理性的，可惜股市裡到處都是韭菜。
